Consulting

With our strategic partner, Unified Human Resources we offer employers a resource to reduce employer-related risks and administrative burden. We begin by offering perspective clients a free HR Risk Assessment.

This assessment helps us to build a picture of your current HR policy and procedures and from that assessment we can discuss what services might work the best for your company.

As part of our HR Consulting service companies are able to work on more of a project basis and may find it convenient for assistance in the following areas:

  • Provide temporary or ongoing access to a particular HR expertise that is not available in house.
  • Need to cover a gap in HR staffing while transitioning HR personnel or adjusting to changes in the companies size and growth
  • Maintain up-to-date policies/employee handbooks
  • Avoid wage and hour violations
  • Hiring and Termination processing
  • Conflict resolution

With the use of our cloud based HR system you can provide ongoing communication and access to your employees to have the latest information regarding key compliance areas as well as forms and the ability to update their personal information.

Our solution is scalable allowing you to quickly and efficiently implement other modules, such as performance management, benefits management, time and attendance and open enrollment.
