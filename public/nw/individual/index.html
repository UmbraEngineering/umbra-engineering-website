What is an Individual Medical/Dental Plan?

Individual insurance plans put your choice first.

When you don’t have employer sponsored benefits or benefits offered through your spouse, individual insurance is a great way to make sure that your healthcare needs are met – on your terms.

We work with all of the major insurance carriers to make sure that you have access to the coverage that best suits your needs.

We also make sure that you understand your benefit to make sure that you are getting the benefit that best matches your needs.

How do I get started?

Start by using our basic checklist:

Identify Your "Must Haves"

  • Medical Coverage?
  • Dental Coverage?
  • Prescription Drugs?

Check the network coverage

  • Are your doctors participating?
  • Do you have a favorite hospital?

Understand your share of the costs

  • Do you understand how copays, coinsurance, and deductibles factor into plan choices? We do!

The most important factors being that you understand how much premium you can afford, and that you don't "over buy".

Answer these questions for yourself, if you aren’t sure about any of these things, we can help you!

Individual Plans and Healthcare Reform

Starting in 2014, most people will be required to have health insurance or pay a penalty if they don’t.

Another option for obtaining an individual plan will be to purchase your coverage through the Oregon Exchange, known as Cover Oregon.

You may also qualify for financial assistance with your premiums. To see if you will qualify please click on the following link:
