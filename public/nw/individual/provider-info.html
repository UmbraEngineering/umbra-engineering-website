Does my Doctor Participate?

Not every doctor participates with every insurance network, and not every insurance carrier uses the same network of providers.

If you want to continue seeing your current doctor and receive the best benefit, you will want to make sure that your doctor participates with the insurance that you are likely to choose.

Below is a list of websites that can be used to determine if your doctor participates with a particular carrier.

What if I travel outside of my area?

If you travel inside of the United States, most carriers have coverage options in place for you when you travel. If this is a primary concern for you, please give us a call and we can help you understand how this effects your coverage.

If you plan on traveling outside of the country, you may want to consider some travel insurance. Travel coverage is typically inexpensive and can help mitigate the risks of traveling internationally.
