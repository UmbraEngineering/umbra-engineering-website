Individual Plan Requirements

For benefit start dates before January 1st, 2014:

Individual Policies are not “Guaranteed Issue” meaning that you can be denied coverage based on your medical history or a pre-existing medical condition. This is one of the most important issues facing people looking for individual coverage today, existing medical issues can mean that you will be denied for individual coverage. Unfortunately, we generally can’t tell you if you qualify or not. You will need to apply in order to find out.

For Benefit start dates after January 1st, 2014:

You will be afforded guarantee issue coverage. This means that you cannot be denied due to a pre-existing medical condition or your medical history. This is a major change to the way that insurance will be provided, but it does nothing to ensure that coverage will be affordable for you so choosing your coverage wisely will be more important than ever – we can help!

Need help paying for your Health Insurance?
