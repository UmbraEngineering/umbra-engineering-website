How to Choose a Plan

It is important that when you begin looking for insurance options that you know the answers to these simple questions. We know that it is complicated, and we are here to help. Feel free to give us a call and we can help you outline answers to these questions.

Identify Your "Must Haves"

  • Do you need medical benefits?
  • Dental Benefits?
  • Prescription Drug coverage?
  • Do you know which carriers are strongest in your area?
  • What price range is right for you?

Know your share of the costs

  • Copays, coinsurance, deductibles etc..
  • Labs & Xrays
  • Prescription Drug Costs

Check the network coverage

  • Is your doctor participating?
  • Do you have a favorite hospital?
  • Do you use alternative care providers?

Don't "Overbuy"

  • How much premium can you afford?
  • How much does deductible affect price?
  • How much financial risk is too much?
