Healthcare Reform – The Basics

The sweeping healthcare reform bill passed in 2010 makes a number of very important changes to the way that health insurance coverage is delivered in the United States.

Among the important changes are changes to who must be insured, who is eligible for government assistance, removal of "pre-existing" condition exclusions and a number of other changes.

Can you get us the best rates?

The rates for groups of 2-50 individuals are set by the state and any licensed agent can get you the same rate. They are based on the demographic and industry of your business and agents don’t get special rates.

The difference is the relationship that you have with your agent. Do they understand your business and work to make sure that your benefits are serving your best interests?

Our goal isn’t to sell you insurance, its to make sure that you are getting the benefits and services that you need to be successful.
