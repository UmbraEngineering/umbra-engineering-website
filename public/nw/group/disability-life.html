Protection for you AND your employees

Disability insurance is designed to help your employees meet their financial obligations when they are sick, injured, or otherwise unable to work.

Call it “paycheck protection” or a way to protect their home since a mortgage payment is generally the most significant expense, either way it offers piece of mind to your employees and to you as a business owner.

Having an income is what makes all of our current lives possible, image the piece of mind that you offer when you have disability insurance as part of your stable of benefits.

Short Term Disability

Short term disability products are offered by many different carriers, one important thing that we help you with is making sure that you are purchasing a policy from a reputable carrier that is financially stable and has a long history of trouble free claims payment.

We work with the best carriers in the industry to ensure you have the best possible coverage.

Long Term Disability

Long term disability insurance is designed to make sure that your employees are financially taken care of in the event of a long term illness or injury that keeps them from working.

It is a piece of mind product that can truly make sure that your employees realize their value to you as employees.

Life Insurance Benefits

Life insurance is a common and inexpensive way to give your employees piece of mind.

By leveraging the buying power of group rates, Employers can help provide financial protection for Employees and their loved ones in the event of an unexpected tragedy.
