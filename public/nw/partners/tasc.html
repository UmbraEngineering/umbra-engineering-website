Strategic Partners

TASC

With over 35 years of industry experience TASC provides third party administration for tax-savings plans, as well as compliance management to cover your employees needs.

Products include:

  • FSA- Flexible Spending Accounts
  • HSA- Health Savings Accounts
  • HRA- Health Reimbursement Accounts
  • FMLA- Family Medical Leave Administration
  • ERISA EDGE- ERISA compliance management

Employees are provided with unique innovations in benefits administration as well as cutting edge technology such as mobile apps and text messaging for the FSA accounts.
