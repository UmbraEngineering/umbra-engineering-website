Reduced Taxes for your employees AND your business!

Many employers don’t realize that the benefits offered under IRS code Section 125 and 106 offer huge tax savings to your employees AND your business.

By offering some of these Tax Savings Plans – such as Premium Only, Flexible Spending, or Transportation Benefits – you can lower the amount of income that the IRS can tax, effectively reducing your taxes while allowing your employees access to some of the most cutting edge reimbursement programs in the country.

Here is how it works!

When employees have an out of pocket cost associated with their qualified benefits package, the IRS allows them to be handled on a “pre-tax” basis. This can be premiums that an employee has to pay (POP Plan), medical costs (Flex Spending), or some other qualifying expenses such as dependent care costs for child care.

When an employees has these qualifying expenses, a plan can be set up to allow employees to make these contributions occur before taxes are applied, effectively lowering the employee’s tax liability. Even better – reduced employee taxable income means that employers pay less in FICA taxes! Win Win!

Transportation & Parking Plan

Some transportation expenses are also eligible for tax free status as part of a “Transportation and Parking plan”. This plan allows employees to be reimbursed for their qualifying expenses – such as parking and transit passes – from pre-tax funds. Again, lowering their tax liability and saving them and their employer money.
