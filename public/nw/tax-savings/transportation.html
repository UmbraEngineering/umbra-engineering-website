What is a Transportation Spending Account?

In short, Transportation Spending Accounts are a pre-tax benefit for transit and parking expenses. They work similarly to other flexible spending accounts in that they allow participants to have funds withheld from their paychecks on a pre-tax basis, and can use these funds to pay for qualifying transportation expenses such as parking, transit passes or vanpooling. These funds are accessed with the use of a convenient debit card.

Tax Savings for Qualified Transportation Expenses

The Transportation Equity Act for the 21st Century (Internal Revenue Code Section 132 and TEA-21) make it possible for employers to allow employee participation in a pre-tax account to pay for qualified work-related commuting and parking expenses.

Some limitations apply, so feel free to contact us for more information.
