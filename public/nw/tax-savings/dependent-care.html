What is a Dependent Care Spending Account?

This account may be used to cover the cost of care for children under age 13 and some other tax qualified dependents.

Participants have funds withheld from their paycheck on a pre-tax basis and use the convenient debit card to pay for their services with these pre-tax dollars – reducing their tax liability and the Employers FICA tax bill at the same time.

Reimbursements can also be made after the fact using a convenient online system with a 24 hour claims processing turnaround.
